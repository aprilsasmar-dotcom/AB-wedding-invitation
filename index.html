Our Vow
A Promise

I choose you, not only in the light,
but in every shadow I have learned to walk through.

I have known what it means to break, and still,
here I am, choosing love again, choosing you.

With you, I am not beginning from nothing,
but from everything I have survived.

We will not promise a life without storms,
but a home within each other.

In laughter and in silence, in certainty and in doubt,
in the quiet days and the overwhelming ones,
I will stand beside you.

Not perfectly, not endlessly,
but real, steady, and growing.

And if love is a journey,
then let this be our brave beginning,
two souls no longer searching,
finally choosing to stay.
